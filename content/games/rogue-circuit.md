# Rogue Circuit

Rogue Circuit is a thrilling roguelike set in a dystopian cyberpunk future. Each run is unique with procedurally generated levels, diverse enemy types, and powerful upgrades.

## Master the System

Master the strategic combat system, experiment with different builds, and see how far you can push into the corrupted network. Every run teaches you something new, and every death makes you stronger.

## Endless Replayability

With procedurally generated levels and over 100 upgrades to discover, no two runs are ever the same. Experiment with different combinations to find powerful synergies.

## Skill-Based Combat

Fast-paced action requires quick reflexes and smart decision-making. Position yourself carefully, time your abilities perfectly, and adapt to each situation.

## Compete and Climb

Take on daily challenges with unique modifiers and compete on global leaderboards. Can you claim the top spot?
