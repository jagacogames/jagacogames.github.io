Slechte Rik (Bad Rik) is a Halloween-themed casual game created during a Game Jam event. Your mission: prevent Rik from turning the world dark forever!

## Game Jam Creation

Born from the creative intensity of a Game Jam, Slechte Rik showcases what can be achieved in a limited timeframe. The game captures the spirit of Halloween with its spooky theme and dark atmosphere.

## The Challenge

As darkness threatens to consume the world, players must act quickly to stop Rik's sinister plans. The casual gameplay makes it accessible to all players while providing an entertaining challenge.

## Play Now

Slechte Rik is available to play directly in your web browser. No downloads or installations required!

## Perfect for Halloween

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming break or a seasonal experience during Halloween, Slechte Rik delivers spooky fun in an accessible browser-based package.
