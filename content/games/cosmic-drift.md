# Cosmic Drift

Cosmic Drift offers a serene journey through the cosmos. Pilot your spacecraft through beautiful nebulas, discover distant planets, and uncover the mysteries of deep space.

## Explore at Your Own Pace

With no combat or time pressure, Cosmic Drift is a meditative experience focused on exploration and wonder. Take your time to appreciate the beauty of the universe and document your discoveries.

## A Universe of Beauty

Experience photo-realistic space environments rendered with cutting-edge graphics technology. From colorful nebulas to distant galaxies, every view is a work of art.

## Your Personal Journey

Customize your ship, plan your route, and create your own story among the stars. The universe is vast, and every player's journey is unique.

## Peaceful Soundscapes

Accompanied by a relaxing ambient soundtrack that enhances the sense of wonder and tranquility as you drift through the cosmos.
