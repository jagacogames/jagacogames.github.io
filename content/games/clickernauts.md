# Clickernauts - Click Your Way Through Space

Clickernauts is an addictive incremental clicker game that takes you on a journey through space. Battle waves of aliens by upgrading your spaceship from simple lasers to powerful Galaxy consumers!

## Incremental Progression

Start with basic equipment and click your way to cosmic dominance. Each click brings you closer to powerful upgrades that transform your humble spacecraft into a galaxy-destroying juggernaut.

## Upgrade Your Arsenal

**Early Game:**
- Basic laser weapons
- Shield improvements
- Hull reinforcements

**Mid Game:**
- Advanced weapon systems
- Automated turrets
- Energy generators

**Late Game:**
- Planet crackers
- Star harvesters
- Galaxy consumers

## Idle Mechanics

Even when you're not actively playing, your ship continues to generate resources. Come back to find your progress has continued, allowing you to purchase even more powerful upgrades.

## Space Combat

Face off against increasingly challenging alien forces. As your ship grows more powerful, so do your enemies. The balance between active clicking and strategic upgrades keeps the gameplay engaging.

## Accessible Gaming

Clickernauts runs directly in your web browser, making it easy to jump in for a quick session or leave running in the background while you work. Perfect for casual gaming moments throughout your day.

## The Ultimate Goal

Can you upgrade your ship enough to consume entire galaxies? Start your journey as a humble space pilot and evolve into a cosmic force of nature. The universe is waiting!
